Frequently Asked Questions

How do I schedule an appointment?

To schedule an appointment, please call our office at (724) 627-0846 during our regular business hours. We recommend scheduling routine wellness exams at least 1-2 weeks in advance.

What should I bring to my pet's first appointment?

Please bring any previous medical records, current medications, a list of any symptoms or concerns, and a fresh stool sample if possible. Dogs should be on leashes and cats should be in secure carriers.

Do you accept walk-ins?

We accept walk-ins for vaccinations only on Wednesdays from 10:00 AM to 2:00 PM. For all other services, an appointment is required.

How can I get my pet's medication refilled?

For medication refills, please call us at least 48 hours in advance. Some medications may require a recent examination before they can be refilled.

Appointment Requests
