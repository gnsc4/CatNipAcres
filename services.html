Our Services

Comprehensive veterinary care for your pets

At Catnip Acres Veterinary Clinic, we provide a wide range of veterinary services for both cats and dogs. Our focus is on delivering high-quality, affordable care to ensure that all pets in our community can receive the medical attention they need.

Our team is committed to treating your pets with compassion and professionalism. We understand that each animal is unique and requires individualized care.

Wellness Exams

Regular check-ups and preventative care to keep your pet healthy. We offer comprehensive physical examinations to detect any potential health issues early.

Our wellness exams include:

  • Thorough physical examination
  • Weight and nutritional assessment
  • Dental health check
  • Discussion of any behavioral concerns
  • Preventative care recommendations

Regular wellness exams are essential for maintaining your pet's long-term health and can help catch potential issues before they become serious problems.

Vaccinations

Protection against common and serious diseases. We provide core and lifestyle vaccinations for both cats and dogs tailored to their specific needs.

For dogs, we offer vaccinations for:

  • Rabies
  • Distemper, Hepatitis, Parainfluenza, and Parvovirus (DHPP)
  • Bordetella (Kennel Cough)
  • Leptospirosis
  • Lyme Disease

For cats, we offer vaccinations for:

  • Rabies
  • Feline Viral Rhinotracheitis, Calicivirus, and Panleukopenia (FVRCP)
  • Feline Leukemia (FeLV)

We provide affordable vaccination packages and offer walk-in vaccination services on Wednesdays from 10:00 AM to 2:00 PM.

Spay & Neuter

Low-cost sterilization services to help control the pet population and improve your pet's health and behavior. We specialize in high-volume/low-cost cat spay and neuter procedures.

Benefits of spaying/neutering include:

  • Prevention of unwanted litters
  • Reduced risk of certain cancers
  • Decreased roaming behavior
  • Reduced aggression in males
  • Prevention of heat cycles in females

We hold scheduled spay & neuter clinics on Fridays and Saturdays. Please contact us to schedule an appointment.

Diagnostic Services

Send-off lab work (blood-work, fecal tests, urinalysis, etc.) and in-house x-rays available at time of appointment, if necessary.

Our diagnostic capabilities include:

  • Complete blood count (CBC)
  • Blood chemistry panels
  • Thyroid testing
  • Urinalysis
  • Fecal testing for parasites
  • Digital radiography (x-rays)
  • FeLV/FIV testing for cats
  • Heartworm testing for dogs

Timely and accurate diagnostics are crucial for developing effective treatment plans for your pet.

Surgical Procedures

We offer a range of surgical procedures with the highest standards of care and safety for your pet. Our clinic is equipped with modern surgical equipment.

Our surgical services include:

  • Spay and neuter procedures
  • Mass removals
  • Wound repair
  • Dental extractions
  • Minor orthopedic procedures
  • Foreign body removal

All surgical patients receive appropriate pain management and careful monitoring throughout their procedure and recovery.

Telehealth Consultations

If not physically present, our veterinarian may be available for tele-health consultations through our certified veterinary technician.

Telehealth services can be useful for:

  • Follow-up consultations
  • Minor concerns that may not require an in-person visit
  • Initial assessment to determine if an in-person visit is necessary
  • Behavioral questions
  • Nutrition and weight management advice

Please call our office to inquire about the availability of telehealth consultations for your specific situation.

Additional Services

Dental Care

Basic dental assessments and care recommendations to maintain your pet's oral health.

Parasite Prevention

Flea, tick, and heartworm prevention products and advice tailored to your pet's needs.

Nail Trims

Regular nail trimming to keep your pet comfortable and prevent complications.

Nutritional Counseling

Advice on appropriate diet and nutrition for your pet's specific life stage and health needs.

Microchipping

Permanent pet identification to help reunite you with your pet if they become lost.

Senior Pet Care

Specialized care for older pets to maintain quality of life and manage age-related conditions.

Pricing Information

At Catnip Acres, we strive to keep our services affordable without compromising on quality. Prices may vary based on your pet's specific needs and condition. Please contact us for current pricing information.

We accept cash, credit cards, debit cards, CareCredit, and Scratchpay to help make veterinary care accessible to all pet owners.
