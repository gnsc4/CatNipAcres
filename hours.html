Appointment Information

Scheduling an Appointment

To schedule an appointment, please call our clinic at (724) 627-0846 during our regular business hours. We recommend scheduling routine wellness exams at least 1-2 weeks in advance.

Arrival Time

Please arrive 10-15 minutes before your scheduled appointment time to complete any necessary paperwork. For first-time visits, please arrive 15-20 minutes early.

Cancellation Policy

We kindly request at least 24 hours' notice for appointment cancellations. This allows us to offer the time slot to another pet in need.

Preparing Your Pet

For dogs, please ensure they are on a leash. For cats, please bring them in a secure carrier. For all pets, it's helpful to bring a list of any current medications and a recent history of any symptoms or concerns.

Holiday Schedule

Catnip Acres Veterinary Clinic is closed on the following holidays:

  • New Year's Day
  • Memorial Day
  • Independence Day
  • Labor Day
  • Thanksgiving Day
  • Christmas Day

We may have reduced hours around holidays. Please call ahead to confirm our schedule if you need care around these times.

Emergency Care

Important: Catnip Acres is not an emergency clinic. If your pet is experiencing a medical emergency outside of our regular hours, please contact one of the following emergency veterinary facilities:

Signs That Require Emergency Care:

Difficulty breathing
Severe bleeding or trauma
Inability to urinate
Seizures
Collapse or unconsciousness
Suspected poisoning
Severe vomiting or diarrhea
Bloated, hard, or painful abdomen
