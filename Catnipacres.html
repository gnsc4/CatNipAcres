About Catnip Acres

Catnip Acres began in 7 as a cat rescue that conducted high-volume/low-cost cat spay and neuter clinics. We've since evolved into a high-quality community care veterinary clinic.

Catnip Acres is proud to provide affordable veterinary care for both cats and dogs, while continuing to honor our original mission for the betterment of the feline population.

Our mission is to ameliorate the suffering associated with cat overpopulation in Southwestern PA, specifically Greene County. Catnip Acres reaches an under-served subset of animals, providing high-volume/low-cost sterilization programs for cats.

Through sterilization and vaccination, Catnip Acres increases animal quality of life and fights overpopulation, thereby decreasing animal abuse, neglect, disease, and shelter over-crowding.

Note: THE CLINIC IS NOT A RESCUE. Please do not contact the clinic about taking cats to put up for adoption, we are not involved in that process. Visit the website catnipacresrescue.org for more information.

Our Services

Wellness Exams

Regular check-ups and preventative care to keep your pet healthy. We offer comprehensive physical examinations to detect any potential health issues early.

Vaccinations

Protection against common and serious diseases. We provide core and lifestyle vaccinations for both cats and dogs tailored to their specific needs.

Spay & Neuter

Low-cost sterilization services to help control the pet population and improve your pet's health and behavior. We specialize in high-volume/low-cost cat spay and neuter procedures.

Diagnostic Services

Send-off lab work (blood-work, fecal tests, urinalysis, etc.) and in-house x-rays available at time of appointment, if necessary.

Surgical Procedures

We offer a range of surgical procedures with the highest standards of care and safety for your pet. Our clinic is equipped with modern surgical equipment.

Telehealth Consultations

If not physically present, our veterinarian may be available for tele-health consultations through our certified veterinary technician.
