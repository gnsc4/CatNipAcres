About Catnip Acres

Catnip Acres began in 2007 as a cat rescue that conducted high-volume/low-cost cat spay and neuter clinics. We've since evolved into a high-quality community care veterinary clinic.

Catnip Acres is proud to provide affordable veterinary care for both cats and dogs, while continuing to honor our original mission for the betterment of the feline population.
