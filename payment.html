Financing Options

CareCredit

CareCredit is a healthcare credit card designed for your health and wellness needs, including veterinary care. It works like a regular credit card but is exclusively for healthcare services.

Benefits include:

  • Special financing options
  • No up-front costs or prepayment penalties
  • Quick and easy application process
Apply for CareCredit

Scratchpay

Scratchpay offers simple payment plans for veterinary care. You can apply online in minutes and get an instant decision. There's no impact on your credit score to check your eligibility.

Features include:

  • Clear payment plans with fixed fees
  • No compounding interest or deferred interest
  • Fast online application

Payment FAQs

Do you accept pet insurance?

Yes, we accept all major pet insurance plans. We'll provide you with a detailed receipt that you can submit to your insurance company for reimbursement. Please note that payment is still due at the time of service.

Can I set up a payment plan?

For routine care, we expect payment at the time of service. For more extensive treatments, we recommend applying for CareCredit or Scratchpay, which offer flexible payment plans.

How do I know what my visit will cost?

We provide estimates for all procedures and treatments before services are rendered. If you have questions about the cost of a specific service, please ask our staff.

Is there a fee for canceled appointments?

We request at least 24 hours' notice for appointment cancellations. While we don't currently charge a cancellation fee, repeated no-shows may affect future appointment availability.

Do you offer discounts for multiple pets?

Yes, we offer multi-pet discounts for certain services. Please ask our staff for details when scheduling appointments for multiple pets.
